About Us

UniFind is a student–centered web application designed to help learners easily locate and retrieve misplaced items within the school campus. Created by a team of BS Computer Science students, UniFind aims to provide a faster, more organized, and more reliable system for handling lost-and-found concerns, replacing the traditional manual process that often leads to delays, confusion, and unclaimed items.

Our mission is to make lost-and-found management simple, transparent, and accessible for all students and staff. By integrating modern web technologies, UniFind offers an efficient platform where users can report lost items, submit found objects, and check updates in real time.

We envision a campus where no item goes permanently missing—where technology bridges the gap between students and their belongings.

The UniFind team is committed to continuous improvement, ensuring that the system remains user-friendly, secure, and helpful to the entire school community.

UniFind Brand Identity

The UniFind logo represents our commitment to providing a reliable, efficient, and student-centered lost-and-found management system. Our brand identity reflects innovation, trust, and accessibility in campus services.

Design Elements & Meaning:

  • Blue Color: Represents trust, reliability, and professionalism in our services
  • Yellow Accent: Symbolizes energy, enthusiasm, and the warmth of helping students
  • Typography: Modern and clean font that ensures readability across all platforms
  • Icon Design: Represents connection, finding, and returning lost items

Brand Values:

Our logo embodies unity, discovery, and progress - core values that drive our mission to revolutionize campus lost-and-found management. It represents our dedication to connecting students with their belongings, ensuring no item goes permanently missing, and embracing technology to enhance the experience for everyone in the campus community.
